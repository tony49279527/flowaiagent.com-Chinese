Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 29, 2026

FlowAI Agent ("we", "our", or "us") takes your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information.

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you register or use our services, we collect your name, email address, and other information you provide.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you use our services, including ASINs you analyze and reports generated.
  • Technical Information: Including your IP address, browser type, device information, etc.

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services

  • Send you reports and service notifications

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We only share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • With service providers (such as payment processing, email delivery)
  • To comply with legal requirements or respond to lawful government requests

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Using HTTPS to encrypt data in transit

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy. When no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymize your data.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve user experience and analyze website traffic. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.
